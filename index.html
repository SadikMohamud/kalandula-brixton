ABOUT US

Nestled in the heart of Brixton, Kalandula brings the vibrant flavors of Angola to London's diverse culinary scene. Named after Angola's majestic Kalandula Falls, our restaurant is a celebration of Angolan-Portuguese fusion cuisine.

Our expert chefs blend traditional Angolan recipes with modern cooking techniques, creating an unforgettable dining experience that honors our heritage while embracing contemporary tastes.
